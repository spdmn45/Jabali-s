About Jabali's

Tradition since 1948

Our story started in 1948, when a proud Palestinian family opened a small restaurant to share the food they grew up loving. From fresh baked bread and sizzling musakhan to sweet knafeh, everything was made just like teta used to make it.

It wasn’t just about food , it was about gathering, laughing, and saying “yalla, eat!” Today, we still cook the same way: simple, warm, and full of love. Ahlan w sahlan, you’re family here.
